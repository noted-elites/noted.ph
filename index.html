Gemma Thirty Nine Returns: BPOO Block Showcases Their Production Prowess

After six months of hiatus, the MSU hit show Gemma Thirty-Nine returns on Facebook Watch starring all of the main and recurring cast from the BPOO block. 

"We missed this," said Gemma Thirty-Nine star and producer Kian Catimbang. "We were so busy with our thesis that we forgot to have fun not just for the sake of grades, but for art for art's sake.''

Gemma Thirty-Nine creator, director, and producer Dyulliana Toyogon also expressed her excitement with their upcoming 6th episode about the Vanguards' Month Celebration. 

"The cast contributed a lot to our (English Department) win," said Toyogon. "We wouldn't have won without the crew's support."
